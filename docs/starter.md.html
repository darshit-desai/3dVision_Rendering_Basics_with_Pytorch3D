Re-creating the Dolly Zoom (15 points) ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- The [Dolly Zoom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dolly_zoom) is a famous camera effect, first used in the Alfred Hitchcock film [Vertigo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G7YJkBcRWB8). The core idea is to change the focal length of the camera while moving the camera in a way such that the subject is the same size in the frame, producing a rather unsettling effect. **On your webpage, include a gif with your dolly zoom effect.**
